About Me

Mechatronics and Controls Engineer with over 10 years of experience in the automotive industry, specializing in the development and implementation of advanced control algorithms for safety-critical systems. Expertise spans embedded systems, functional safety (ISO 26262), vehicle dynamics, and electric motor control. Proven ability to lead projects from concept to production, including requirements definition, system architecture design, software development, and validation. Currently focused on mastering PMSM Field Oriented Control (FOC) techniques, leveraging TI's Fast Current Loop (FCL) for high-performance motor control applications.
